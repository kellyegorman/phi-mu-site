PHILANTHROPIC EVENTS:

Our sisters participate in service year-long, within our philanthropies and other community opportunities. Our chapter also offer several dedicated events to raise awareness and funds for CMN Hospitals and UH Rainbow Babies.

MIRACLE BALL

Each spring, we invite community members, family, and alumni to celebrate our philanthropy at our annual Miracle Ball. During this event, we meet families supported by CMN Hospitals, listen to guest speakers discuss the impact of our philanthropies, and we participate in charity raffles.

MIRACLE MAN

Fall 2023 marked our first annual Miracle Man competition. This competition features representatives of several large CWRU organizations. During this competition, participants compete for the title of Phi Mu's Miracle Man, and receive votes in the form of donations for our philanthropies.
