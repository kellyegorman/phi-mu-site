Day One, Panhellenic



Get to know all ten panhellenic sororities. Spend twenty minutes each in ten different parties. On this day, we'll briefly get to know each other and why sorority recruitment is meaningful to us.

Week 1, Saturday!

Day Two, Philanthropy


Go to up to eight parties for thirty minutes each. On this day, we will share why we love our philanthropies--CMN Hospitals, UH Rainbow Babies, and the Phi Mu Foundation-- along with ways we serve our communities!

Week 1, Sunday

Day Three, Sisterhood

On day three, you will attend up to five parties for 50 minutes each. This day is all about what makes Phi Mu special. Learn about our connections with the chapter, its values, and each other.

Week 2, Saturday!

Day Four, Preference


This is the final day! You will go to up to two parties for one hour each. After this, you will connect with your Rho Gamma for one final time in order to sign the MRABA and submit your final ranking!

Week 2, Sunday!
